TAILORING AND KNITTING

Learning a skill is helpful for the young learners. This can shape their career in the future. It is very important to set up programs besides the normal class lessons for them to learn and practice a skill for their survival. No one knows for sure how this can turn out to be.

Life experience shows that children who learned a skill while in school can do better while adult than those who only focused on normal classroom lessons. We are determined to equip our learners with life saving skills while learning. This time is very crucial for these young learners.

The availability of many online courses is a great opportunity for many young persons to learn a skill that can turn out to be a life changing experience in life. We encourage all young boys and girls to learn a skill that will help support themselves to be successful in life. It's all about courage and determination to succeed.

CONSTRUCTION OF CLASSROOMS

The construction of classrooms are undergoing at the organiztion's educational center at Lweza Mercy Nursery and Primary School site. Since the number of learners is increasing it is a matter of urgency to construct classrooms to improve on the learning environment of our young learners. In order for the children at MPS to learn properly, the school needs classrooms as well as furniture to use such as desks, tables, capboards, and trained teachers. The school is currently running 9 class rooms, 3 classes for nursery section and 7 classes for primary section. However, there is a great need for classrooms where children need to receive education in good environment.

CHILD SPONSORSHIP PROGRAM

These Children need your help to receive basic education. They need a bright future. Once educated, they will fight poverty through knowledge and hard work. Any donation can make a difference and cause a change! This program helps individual children to get individual sponsors to support their education. The sponsor receives updates from the child, typically including photos and translated letters, which help create the feeling of a personal relationship with the child. The sponsorship amount covers all the needs of the sponsored child including, school fees, a set of uniform with sports wear, and sweaters. Our organization is appealing to all the good Samaritans out there to support vulnerable children and orphans in their academic journey. Currently CSF is providing education to more than 500 children with evident need for support. Help one and make a difference!! To support one child or a group of children, please fill out our Contact us Form and submit it to us for processing and information sharing for you to start supporting some of our needy children to receive education at school.
